Meet Boris & Kathy

Two of the most recognized names in currency and futures trading, published authors and regular guests on CNBC and Bloomberg.

Boris Schlossberg

Co-Founder · Futures & Prop Trading

Boris began his Wall Street career over two decades ago at Drexel Burnham Lambert, trading everything from equities and options to index futures and FX. He served as Senior Currency Strategist at FXCM and Director of Currency Research at GFT before co-founding BK with Kathy Lien. A weekly contributor to CNBC's Squawk Box and author of Technical Analysis of the Currency Market and Millionaire Traders, Boris built the momentum-based techniques behind BK's strategies, and trades them live every day.

Kathy Lien

Co-Founder · The Queen of Macro Forex Trader

The Queen of Macro graduated from NYU Stern at just 18 and became the youngest trader ever at JPMorgan, starting on the interbank FX desk before moving to cross-markets proprietary trading. She launched DailyFX.com as Chief Strategist in 2003 and co-founded BK with Boris. A CNBC regular quoted by the Wall Street Journal, Reuters and Bloomberg, Kathy is the author of Day Trading and Swing Trading the Currency Market and her newest book, Prop Trading Secrets. Her blend of macro insight and chart precision is the backbone of BK's strategies.
